What to expect at Beantage Café?

Blogged by: Diane Kathleen Viva


Well, here's the good news for all the coffee drinkers and comfort seekers out there! No need to go to the metro to catch up for a nice cup of coffee. Surely, Beantage Coffee Shop does it better.

This new cozy place is located at Imus, Cavite, beside Banahaw Spa. This dainty cafe was brought to life by a business-minded and dedicated couple named Lovely and Andrew. Their business was a product of both experience and expertise. Beantage Coffee Shop offers wide range of cold beverages, coffee, pastas, nachos, sandwiches, sweet pastries and even rice meals. As much as possible, they try to introduce delicious fusion beverages that are uniquely them. Their coffee is made special with different brewing procedures that makes it even more flavorful. They support Filipino-produced coffee beans. Beantage Coffee Shop ensures that quality exceeds the cost. Thus, despite being new in the business, they strive to create and innovate more that will surely tease your taste buds.

For the price sensitive customers out there don't worry because everything is budget friendly. Beantage Coffee Shop wants to be the place for leisure, dining, bonding and everything fun in between. This is proven by the vintage vibe brought about by the store atmospherics. The walls are painted with uplifting quotes that will make you enjoy even more with every sip and bite.

Beantage Coffee Shop is surely the place to be! So gather up your friends, family, classmates, workmates and everyone whom you would want to spend a free time sipping a good cup of Beantage coffee. There's no better way to cap off a stressful day than stopping by at Beantage Coffee Shop for "A little love in every cup".
